Instructions

The objective of the game is to match the "points to match" number by clicking the jewels. The jewels for each round will have different value ranging from 1-12. It's your job to figure out how many points are in each specific jewel for each round. If you match the points together you win, but if your points go over the points to match... you lose :( Good Luck!!!
